Welcome to UOK Computer Club

The journey of UOK Computer Club (UOKCC) started on 13th September 2012 with authorization from the Office of Student Affairs (OSA) of UOK. Realizing the key importance to adapt along with upgrading technology, six chief founding members introduced this club with its mission and vision all set. The goal was to build up a strong community of UOK Computer Club students by offering them a potential platform that deals with technology and its advancement. Why Join UOK Computer Club?

To build skills in various sectors including Content Writing, Photojournalism, Video Editing, Event Organizing, Volunteering, Development, UI/UX Design, and Graphic Design.
To connect with a community of senior, junior, alumni, and respected teachers. Build harmony and get ideas about the demanding skills of the current and future professional domain
To organize events national events such as Computer Science Fest, Computer Gaming Fest and intra UOKCC events like Jarvis - An Intra UOKCC Carnival and participate in regularly organized workshops and seminars for UOKCC member
